  What's New
Attendance Checking Now Available on both iPhone and Android

The CampusGroups "Attendance Checking" feature is now available on Android as well as iPhone. You can now check-in attendees by searching names, scanning their QR codes or swiping their Student ID, straight from your Android Phone.

What's New
QR Code Scanning

To make it easier for students to access their tickets and QR codes, we've added QR Codes in the event registration confirmation email and event reminder email. Now, no need to hunt for your QR code.
 
  What's New
Online Document Editor

Student leaders can easily edit Word, PowerPoint and Excel files from within Campusgroups with our online editor. Our check-out/check-in system prevents multiple users from creating simultaneous updates overlapping each other.
What's New
Customize Support

Each school has unique ways to handle support, so now you can customize the Support pop-up content. Add your own instructions and links to custom support materials!
  
  What's New
All the form answers files in one click

It's even easier to collect all uploaded documents from surveys and forms: constitutions, business plans, resumes and more. All of your files can now be compressed into one ZIP file for you to download in just one click!
What's New
Edit Widgets Directly While Browsing

No need to edit the webpage in the management section to change your website content! Just click the "Edit" button on the widget itself when browsing. This makes custom widgets much more attractive and fun to use!

You can also add photos to custom widgets! Position on top or on the side of your text.
